About Us

Expert Academy Mumbai is a trusted and well-established educational institute offering high-quality Home Tuition and Online Tuition services. Our mission is to provide personalized, one-to-one learning support that helps students strengthen their concepts and achieve outstanding academic performance.

We provide coaching for CBSE, ICSE, SSC, HSC, and also for competitive exams including JEE, NEET, Olympiads, and aptitude tests. Our team includes highly qualified and experienced teachers who understand each student's learning style and teach accordingly.

In our classes:
✔ Students receive one-to-one personal attention
✔ Regular tests and doubt-solving sessions are conducted
✔ Progress reports are shared with parents
✔ Student’s speed, accuracy, confidence, and concept clarity are improved



Home Tuition Services

Online Tuition Services

Our Online Tuition classes are conducted through Zoom or Google Meet using digital whiteboards, screen sharing, and interactive teaching methods. This mode ensures high-quality learning from the comfort of home.

✔ Live one-to-one online sessions
✔ Recorded video for revision
✔ Digital notes, worksheets and assignments
✔ Weekly tests and performance tracking

Online Tuition is ideal for students who prefer flexible timings and want to learn from expert teachers without traveling.
